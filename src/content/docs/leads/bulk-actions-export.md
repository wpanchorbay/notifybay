As your store grows, your waitlists and wishlists will scale. Managing leads one by one becomes impossible. NotifyBay provides bulk tools to help you manage data efficiently, even with thousands of subscribers.

## Managing Large Lists with Bulk Actions

If you need to change the status of multiple leads or clean up your database, use the Bulk Actions dropdown located above the Leads table.

1.  **Filter/Search First (Optional):** If you only want to affect a specific segment, use the email search bar first.
2.  **Select Leads:** Use the checkboxes on the left side of the table to select the specific leads you want to modify. You can select all leads on the *current page* (up to 20 at a time) by clicking the master checkbox in the table header.
3.  **Choose Action:** Click the "Bulk Actions" dropdown menu. Available actions include:
    *   **Mark Active:** Reset previously notified or expired leads back to active status. Useful if you restocked an item but want to send *another* notification later.
    *   **Mark Expired:** Manually expire leads so they no longer receive notifications. Great for clearing out very old requests.
    *   **Delete:** Permanently remove the records from your database to maintain GDPR compliance or save server space.
4.  **Apply:** Click the **Apply** button to execute the action on all selected leads.

## Exporting to CSV: The Marketing Value

The data you collect in NotifyBay is incredibly valuable for your broader marketing efforts. Even if a customer doesn't convert immediately through an automated notification, they have handed you a highly qualified expression of interest.

To export your data:
1.  Click the **Export CSV** button located in the top right corner of the Leads page.
2.  A `.csv` file containing your entire subscriber database will automatically download to your computer.

### How to Use Your Exported Leads
Once you have your CSV, you can leverage it across your entire marketing stack:

*   **Email Marketing (Mailchimp, Klaviyo, ActiveCampaign):** Import these leads into your main newsletter list. Since you know exactly which product they wanted, you can send highly targeted email campaigns.
*   **Retargeting Ads (Meta/Facebook Ads, Google Ads):** Upload the CSV as a Custom Audience. You can run ads specifically showing the product they waitlisted, reminding them to come back and purchase.
*   **Lookalike Audiences:** Use the exported list to create a Lookalike Audience on Facebook or TikTok. The algorithm will find new potential customers who share similar traits with people who have high intent to buy from your store.
*   **Demand Forecasting:** Share the export with your purchasing or manufacturing team. The sheer volume of waitlist requests is the best indicator of what inventory you should order next.
