## 🔑 Your Account Dashboard

The [NotifyBay.com](https://notifybay.com) customer dashboard is your secure portal for managing the technical side of your subscription.

![NotifyBay Account Dashboard](/notifybay-docs/assets/screenshots/resources-account-dashboard.png)

From your dashboard, you can perform the following essential tasks:

1.  **Download Updates:** Always ensure you are running the latest version of the plugin for maximum performance and compatibility with the latest WooCommerce versions.
2.  **Manage Licenses:** View your active license keys and manage which of your staging or production sites are authorized.
3.  **View Billing:** Access your full purchase history and download professional VAT/tax invoices for your business records.

## 🛠 Premium Technical Support

If you encounter an issue that this guide doesn't resolve, our dedicated support team is available to assist you.

### How to Get Help:
1.  **Open a Ticket:** Log into your account at [NotifyBay.com/support](https://notifybay.com/support).
2.  **Provide Context:** For the fastest resolution, please include:
    *   A brief description of the issue.
    *   A screenshot of your [System Health](/notifybay-docs/settings/system-status/) tab.
    *   Your current WooCommerce and PHP versions (also found on the Status page).

![Support Ticket Form Section](/notifybay-docs/assets/screenshots/resources-support-form.png)

> [!NOTE]
> We typically respond to all premium support inquiries within **24 business hours**.
