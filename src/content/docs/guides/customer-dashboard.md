NotifyBay integrates seamlessly with the standard WooCommerce **My Account** dashboard. This provides a professional experience for your customers, allowing them to track and manage their own notification requests without needing to contact your support team.

## Overview

When a customer is logged into their account on your store, NotifyBay automatically adds new tabs to their account menu. This gives them full transparency into the products they are interested in.

- **Waitlist Tab:** Displays all active back-in-stock notification requests.
- **Wishlist Tab:** Displays all active price-drop notification requests.

## The Waitlist Tab

In the **Waitlist** section, customers can see a clean table of every product they are waiting for.

The table includes:
- **Product Image & Name:** A clear reminder of what they wanted.
- **Current Status:** Whether they are still "Active" or if they have already been "Notified".
- **Actions:** A "Remove" button allowing them to manually cancel a request if they are no longer interested.

## The Wishlist Tab

The **Wishlist** section functions similarly to the waitlist but focuses on price alerts.

<!-- Insert My Account Wishlist Screenshot Here -->

In this view, customers can see:
- **The Product:** The item they are tracking.
- **Target Price:** The specific price they are waiting for.
- **Current Price:** A real-time update of the product's current price, helping them see how close it is to their goal.
- **Manage Alerts:** Option to update their target price or remove the alert entirely.

## Customer Privacy & Control

Providing these tools builds trust with your shoppers. Instead of feeling "trapped" in a notification loop, they have the power to:
1. **Self-Service:** They can opt-out of specific product alerts at any time.
2. **Review Intent:** Seeing their full wishlist in one place often encourages them to complete a purchase, even if the target price hasn't been met yet.
3. **Data Clarity:** Customers can verify which email address they used for their notifications.

## Setup & Requirements

There is **zero configuration** required to enable these tabs. As long as NotifyBay is active, the tabs will appear automatically for logged-in users. 

> [!TIP]
> If you don't see the "Wishlist" tab in My Account, ensure that the **Wishlist feature is enabled** in your [General Settings](/notifybay-docs/settings/general-display/).
